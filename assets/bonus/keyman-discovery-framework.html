Keyman Discovery Framework

Use this five-phase flow in your next meeting with a business owner. Each phase builds safety before you discuss funding structures.

The five discovery phases

Phase 1 — Connect (2 min)
Thank them for their time. Position yourself as a business continuity advisor, not a product presenter.
Phase 2 — Discover (3 min)
"If we looked at your entire company directory today, who is the one person whose empty chair tomorrow would keep you up at night?"
Phase 3 — Explore (2 min)
"If that key person were gone tomorrow, what happens to client contracts, credit lines, and bank guarantees?"
Phase 4 — Quantify (2 min)
"How much real financial loss does the company sustain while you search, recruit, train, and ramp up a replacement?"
Phase 5 — Transition (1 min)
"Would it help to review corporate funding structures that secure that exposure using off-balance-sheet reserves?"

Quantification checklist

  • Annual compensation of the key person
  • Salary multiple (5× conservative · 7× standard · 10× rainmaker)
  • Recruitment and training replacement cost
  • Revenue or profit at risk during disruption
  • Loan covenants or personal guarantees tied to the key person
